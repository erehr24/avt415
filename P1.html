P1.1 Project Ideas

Shroomie's

Shroomie’s is an organic mushroom coffee- a blend of ground medicinal mushrooms and coffee beans. Shroomie’s shares the mushroom’s benefits in an easy way, in everyone’s favorite morning drink.

Chalked Up

Chalked Up is a women owned and operated crochet company- focused on creating unique and original crochet bags for climbers. Using environmentally friendly wool and cotton, our chalk bags help climbers in more ways than on the rock.

Third Eye Mugs

Third Eye Mugs focuses on creating ceramic and stone mugs that help elevate your morning coffee, tea, or warm drink. Hand-thrown mugs of all shapes and sizes, these mugs are inspired and capture the energy of the outdoors.
